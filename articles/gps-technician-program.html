BESITS Launches New GPS Technician Certification Program

January 30, 2025 3 min read BESITS Team
Back to Blog

BESITS — Berntecho Electronic Security & IT Solutions — has officially launched a hands-on GPS Technician Certification Program from our Afienya training centre. Designed for young Ghanaians seeking a practical career in the booming vehicle tracking and fleet management industry, the 4-week program takes participants from zero experience to job-ready certification.

Program duration: 4 weeks · Location: Afienya, Greater Accra · Certificate issued on completion

About the Program

Ghana's transportation and logistics sector is growing rapidly, and so is the demand for GPS tracking systems. Businesses, government agencies, schools, and private individuals are increasingly relying on real-time vehicle tracking — but there is a shortage of trained technicians who can install, configure, and maintain these systems.

BESITS created this program to bridge that gap. Our GPS Technician Certification is not a classroom-only course. From day one, participants work with live GPS tracking devices, SIM cards, online platforms, and real vehicles. By the end of the program, graduates are ready to serve clients professionally and confidently.

What You'll Learn

  • Device hardware: Understanding different GPS tracker models — hardwired, OBD-II, and battery-powered types
  • SIM & network setup: Configuring SIM cards, APN settings, and mobile data for reliable tracking
  • Platform configuration: Setting up tracking dashboards, live maps, alerts, and reports on popular platforms
  • Vehicle installation: Safely mounting and concealing GPS devices in cars, trucks, and motorcycles
  • Geo-fencing: Creating virtual boundaries and configuring automatic alerts for boundary violations
  • Client handover: Teaching clients to use their tracking system and providing professional documentation
  • Troubleshooting: Diagnosing and resolving common GPS tracker issues in the field

Who Should Attend

This program is ideal for:

  • Recent SHS or tertiary graduates looking for a technical career
  • Vehicle mechanics who want to expand their service offerings
  • IT technicians looking to move into field security work
  • Entrepreneurs planning to launch a GPS tracking business
  • Security officers and fleet managers wanting to self-manage tracking

No prior experience in electronics or IT is required. If you have basic smartphone and computer skills, you can succeed in this program.

Certificate & Career Outlook

Upon successful completion of practical assessments and a final project, participants receive a BESITS GPS Technician Certificate — recognised by our network of clients and partner businesses across Ghana.

Graduates have gone on to:

  • Join the BESITS field technician team
  • Start their own GPS installation businesses
  • Get hired by logistics and transport companies needing in-house GPS support
  • Expand to related fields such as CCTV installation and smart home systems

The GPS tracking market in Ghana is growing. Companies managing vehicle fleets — from minibus operators to mining firms — are actively seeking qualified technicians. This certification puts you at the front of that opportunity.

Spaces are limited each intake. Register now to secure your place on the next cohort and begin your career as a GPS Technician.
